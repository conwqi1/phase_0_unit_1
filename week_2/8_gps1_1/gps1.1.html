  • What is pair programming?

    • Pair programming is when you program on a single display with another person.

  • Why pair programming

    • Pair programming offers a way for both to participate to learn from each other and have a sense of a real working situation.

  • How to pair program

    • Pair programming made up by a driver and navigator. The driver is charge of typing and the navigator gives ideas to the driver. The driver and navigator working set forces us to articulate our thoughts.
